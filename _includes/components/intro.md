Sungchan Choi is a researcher at the Korea Electronics Technology Institute (KETI) in South Korea. His work focuses on practical intelligent systems built around IoT platforms, embedded software, connected devices, and real-world digital services.

His background includes research on interoperability for oneM2M and IoT systems, smart environments, and connected farm platforms for smart agriculture. This site presents an English overview of his background, interests, and selected work.
