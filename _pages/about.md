## Professional Profile

Sungchan Choi works on bridging research and deployment: connecting devices, platforms, and services into systems that can operate in practical environments. His work has covered smart agriculture, connected spaces, and software platforms designed for interoperability and extensibility.

KETI, the Korea Electronics Technology Institute, is one of South Korea's leading applied research institutes in electronics and ICT. This page serves as a simple English-language introduction for collaborators, partners, and international visitors.
